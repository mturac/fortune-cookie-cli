## Quick Wins

1. **Funny fortunes are key**
   - Make them relatable
   - Make them screenshot-worthy
   - Mix of wisdom and humor

2. **Visual appeal matters**
   - Beautiful terminal output
   - ASCII art cookie
   - Colorful personalities

3. **Easy to try**
   - One-line install
   - Works immediately
   - No configuration needed

4. **Shareable by design**
   - Every output is tweetable
   - Built-in share command
   - Image generation

## Risk Mitigation

**Risk:** Not viral enough
**Solution:** Focus on roast personality, it's inherently funny

**Risk:** Too simple
**Solution:** Position as "delightfully simple", not "lacking features"

**Risk:** Novelty wears off
**Solution:** Weekly fortune updates, community submissions
